Nail Studio Mila

Individual project, 2025

For this project, I worked on the branding and website development of nailstudiomila.nl, a nail studio. I was responsible for the full web design and visual identity of the brand, ensuring a clean and professional online presence.

In addition, I manage the social media accounts by creating engaging photo and video content, including editing and scheduling. I also assist with marketing-related tasks to support the business's visibility and growth.

This project allowed me to apply a variety of skills including branding, UI/UX design, content creation, and digital strategy — making it a great real-world experience in managing a complete online presence for a small business.
